Icons are Nerd Font glyphs mapped in data/icons.yaml. The theme ships a default set; override by placing your own data/icons.yaml in the site root.

Adding icons

Add entries to data/icons.yaml as name: codepoint pairs:

docker: f308
kubernetes: fd31

Codepoints are hex without a 0x prefix. Browse available glyphs at nerdfonts.com/cheat-sheet .
